Should I call a plumber or restoration team first?

If the leak is active, stop water supply and call for immediate restoration response while arranging plumbing repair. Both are often needed in parallel.

Can plumbing leaks cause mold growth quickly?

Yes. Persistent moisture in enclosed materials can trigger microbial growth, which is why extraction and drying should begin immediately.
